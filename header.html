About Me
Balloons have always been apart of me and my gift but I would've never imagined getting the opportunity to make them my business. Even at the age of 15, I had the privilege to lear the basics of clowning and ballooning in preparation for a mission trip. Decades later, and a few amazing friends gained, like Teresa Forst, Shades the Clown, and Tammy Rosecrans, from Butterfly Kisses, I get to use everything that I have learned. Celebrations and ballooning make up a large part of my life and through the COVID-19 pandemic I have been presented with a lot of opportunities. Moods lowered, and celebrating wasn't as easy however we were able to be a part of more and bring more smiles than we ever thought. Through yard signs and getting to be a apart of things like drive through parades I got to do what I want more than ever. What I want is not just to make things look pretty, while that is nice, I love to make more of balloons and actually fill hearts with joy which is why I love working with charities and churches or fundraisers to accomplish just that. Balloons and decorations are a lot more than just static objects, They can bring joy and not just to children, but to everyone looking to have a fun celebration. I love being presented with all opportunities given and making the most of everything.
Liz Voss
Balloon Artist
